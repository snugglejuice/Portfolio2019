20Minutes Redesign

How to increases visitors' reading time ?

Overview

For my web design class, I had to choose a famous website and Redesigning it by choosing an angle of improvement or changement. I choose to redesign a news website because I have a personal aversion against them. I always find that they display to much information, a lot of sugested articles are unrelevant with what I want to read. Futhermore, I often spend more time to chose which articles, I will read than actually read. That why the problematic that I choose was : "How to increases the visitors'reading time of 20Minutes ?"

Role

Web design project - University project

For redesign, I have create personas, moodboard, create low and high fidelity wireframe and prototype it.

20Minutes Redesign

Problems

As I don't work for 20Minutes and don't have access to their statistics, all the problems that I raised are my own point of view of the subject and may not be a problem for a majority of users

20 Minutes website may loose the users due to different reasons and I selected 2 problems :

  • User can feel lost and don't know what to read in front of the amount of articles
  • Irrelevant content for the user

Goals

  1. Reduce the time of article's choice for the users
  2. Increase the users' reading time
  3. Create a reader loyalty to 20Minutes website

My redesign idea

Based on the name of the website "20Minutes", I decided to make my redesigned by thinking about time. What I wanted was to create an interface with which the user can choose faster an article which interest him and reading it, in knowing how much time he needs to read this article. The important thing is that the suggested articles have to be based on the user interest and in a second time on the time the user have.

With this idea in mind, I begin to work on the concept and design.

Color and Typography

For the typography, I choose to use Merriweather regular for the title because I wanted to use a serif font which remind paper journals. For the content, I decided to use Muli as it's a simple and easy to read font.
